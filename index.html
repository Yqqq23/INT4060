Why Linear Algebra Matters

Foundation of Mathematics

Linear algebra is the backbone of modern mathematics, essential for understanding multidimensional spaces and solving complex systems of equations.

Powers Machine Learning

Every neural network, computer vision algorithm, and AI system relies on linear algebra operations for processing and learning from data.

Graphics & Animation

From video games to animated movies, linear transformations make it possible to rotate, scale, and project objects in 3D space.

Data Science

Analyze datasets, reduce dimensionality, and extract patterns using powerful matrix operations and vector spaces.
